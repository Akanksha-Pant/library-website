About

The MBA Library was established in 2022 with a small assortment of books and journals. Currently, the library boasts an extensive collection of textbooks, reference books, journals, articles, audio-visual materials, CDs, and case studies. All students, faculty members, and employees of the institute have the privilege of utilizing the library's facilities, which are integrated online through KOHA. The MBA Library is fully automated and primarily focuses on management and related subjects. It houses a diverse array of resources, both in print and electronic formats, such as books, journals, databases, audio-visual materials, CDs/DVDs, e-journals, reports, case studies, conference proceedings, and training manuals. With its modern collection of knowledge resources and innovative information services, the library plays a vital role in supporting the intellectual pursuits of the academic community. As a hybrid library, the MBA Library incorporates cutting-edge technological applications. The entire library collection, including CD-ROM databases and online databases, can be accessed through the institute's network.
